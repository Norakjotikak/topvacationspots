TOP VACATION SPOTS

Top Vacation Spots You Need to Visit in Your Life Time

By: Jotikak
The world is full of fascinating places. Planning the perfect vacation involves packing up, leaving home, and experiencing something new.

1. Koh Rong, Cambodia

Koh Rong is an island in the Sihanoukville Province of Cambodia.Koh Rong is the second largest island of Cambodia.

Top Attraction
  • Fishing
  • Snorkelling
  • Diving

2. Seim Reap, Cambodia

Siem Reap is the second-largest city of Cambodia, as well as the capital and largest city of Siem Reap Province in northwestern Cambodia.

Top Attraction
  • Visit Angkor Wat
  • Visit Pub Street at night
  • Restaurants and Dining

3. Phú Quốc, Vietnam

Phú Quốc is a Vietnamese island off the coast of Cambodia in the Gulf of Thailand. Phú Quốcis the largest island in Vietnam.

Top Attraction
  • Take a ride in cable car
  • Dolphin Show
  • Check out Phú Quốc beach
